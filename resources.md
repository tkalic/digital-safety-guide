# Resources & Tools

A curated list — not exhaustive, but vetted. Everything here is worth your time.

---

## Privacy & Security Tools

| Tool | What it does | Cost |
|------|-------------|------|
| [Bitwarden](https://bitwarden.com) | Password manager, open-source | Free |
| [ProtonMail](https://proton.me) | Encrypted email | Free tier |
| [ProtonVPN](https://protonvpn.com) / [Mullvad](https://mullvad.net) | VPN | Paid |
| [Signal](https://signal.org) | Encrypted messaging | Free |
| [uBlock Origin](https://ublockorigin.com) | Ad and tracker blocker | Free |
| [Firefox](https://firefox.com) / [Brave](https://brave.com) | Privacy-focused browsers | Free |
| [Have I Been Pwned](https://haveibeenpwned.com) | Check if your email appeared in a breach | Free |

## Learning & Further Reading

### Data Privacy
- [Electronic Frontier Foundation](https://eff.org) — digital rights advocacy and practical guides
- [Mozilla Privacy Blog](https://foundation.mozilla.org) — accessible privacy news and research
- [GDPR full text](https://gdpr-info.eu) — if you want to read the actual regulation

### AI Ethics
- [AI Now Institute](https://ainowinstitute.org) — research on social implications of AI
- [UNESCO AI Ethics](https://en.unesco.org/artificial-intelligence) — international framework
- [EU AI Act summary](https://artificialintelligenceact.eu) — readable breakdown of the regulation

### Cybersecurity
- [TryHackMe](https://tryhackme.com) — hands-on beginner labs
- [HackTheBox](https://hackthebox.com) — intermediate to advanced practice
- [OWASP Top 10](https://owasp.org/www-project-top-ten/) — web security essentials
- [Krebs on Security](https://krebsonsecurity.com) — reliable security journalism

### Digital Sustainability
- [Green Web Foundation](https://thegreenwebfoundation.org) — check if sites run on green hosting
- [Website Carbon Calculator](https://websitecarbon.com) — estimate a site's footprint
- [iFixit](https://ifixit.com) — repair guides for electronics

### Fact-Checking
- [Snopes](https://snopes.com)
- [FactCheck.org](https://factcheck.org)
- [Reuters Fact Check](https://reuters.com/fact-check)
